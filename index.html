The Coders Guild

Promoting inclusion, diversity and equality through exceptional training, coaching and CPD opportunities for the software industry. A safe space offering community-driven, peer-reviewed open-source, knowledge share.

Mission

Promoting:

  • Diversity and inclusion in the industry
  • Accessible code as standard
  • Best practice and knowledge share
  • Mentoring and apprenticeships

What We Do

  • Web Dev Apprenticeship

    We recruit people, mainly from underrepresented groups, and train and coach tehm through an 18 omth work placement.

  • Leeds Web Dev Meetup

    A community of web designers and developers looking to connect and share with others in the Leeds City Region. We are especially keen to meet people new to the industry.

  • Accessibility Code Sprints

    We run 2 day code sprints for open source projects with a focus on improving accessibility. We also run free training for people who want to get into contributing to open source projects.

  • Event Support

    We love to support events and initiatives that match our mission. Get in touch with Crispin via Twitter.
